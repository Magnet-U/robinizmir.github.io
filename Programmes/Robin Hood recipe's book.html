Vegetarian carbonara

Sorry, not really the good picture but doesn't matter, does it?
  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
onions 1
carrots 2
pasta 800g
pecorino 100g
eggs 4

Step 1: Boil the water and, when that is done, add the pasta.

Step 2: Cut the carrots and the onion in small pieces, cook them on a plate

Step 3: Scramble the eggs in a basket, add the pecorino

Step 4: Add everything together, serve warm with abundant wine

Source: From here, adjusted with what my old granny taught me

Risotto

I wanted to find some John Lawless soldiers but then it would not really seem a receipe's book isn't it?
  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity Specifications
onions 1/2
mushrooms 200g
rice 500g
John Landless soldiers ass 300g (if available)
salt a pinch

Step 1: Boil the water and, when that is done, add the rice.

Step 2: Cut the mushrooms and the onion in small pieces, cook them on a plate

Step 3: Also cut the ass of john's soldiers, fry and set in ACAB shape

Step 4: Add everything together, serve warm with rum and coke
